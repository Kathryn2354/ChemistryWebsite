This is a spectrometer project by Dr. Sherrer.

This project is collaborated with student taking computer science classes. We have create a website for people to upload the results.

This is how to use the cdspeccollab.smcm.edu website:

If you want to upload a CVS file to that website, please click on the upload button.

This will lead you to a create page, which will have certain requirement you will need to fill out first.

Once you fill in all of the required info in the create page, you can then click summit button.

Click on the index button on the navagation bar to take you the table view page:

If you want to multigraph a graph, you must select the graph that data info first before you click on the multigraph button.

There is an search bar in the table view page.

There is an previous button and next button in the table view page at the bottom, so you see how many people put entries in the website.

If you want to create an account on the website. Click on the sign up button on the navagation bar to take you the sign up page:

This will lead you to a sign up page, which will have certain requirement you need to fill out first.

Once you fill in all of the required info in the sign up page, you can then click summit button.

You must go in your email and click on the varification link in order to verify your account.

You are done with signing up for the website.

If you want to sign in to the website. Click on the sign in button on the navagation bar to take you the sign in page:

Your login username will be the beginning part of the your email without email ending part.

You need to enter the verifyed password you created for your account on the website.

You can make the choice of allowing the website to remember your account if you like, this part is optional.

You are done with signing in to the website.

What happen if you forget your account password?:

Click on the forgot password button at the bottom of the Sign In page.

This will take to a password reset page where you can reset your account password.

Once you enter the password reset page, you need to enter the email address and press summit button that can reset you password.

You need to go to your email and click on the reset password link in order to change your account password.

You are done with resetting your account password.
